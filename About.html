  • Agricultural Heritage

    Solsona is a town known for its fertile lands and abundant water resources, which support a strong agricultural sector. The festival highlights this by honoring the cultivation of crops, especially the cherished root crop "aba-daludal".

  • Abaludal Dance

    A central feature is the "Abaludal Dance," which depicts the entire farming cycle, from planting and weeding to harvesting and preparation of the aba.
